Welcome to the Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department at SSTX is dedicated to providing cutting-edge education and research opportunities in computing. Our program emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical applications, preparing students for successful careers in technology.

With a diverse faculty of industry professionals and academic researchers, we offer comprehensive programs from introductory computing to specialized graduate-level courses in artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and more.
