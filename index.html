Welcome to the Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department at SSTX High School is dedicated to providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in our increasingly digital world. Our curriculum emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical applications, preparing students for college and future careers in technology.

Whether you're just beginning your coding journey or already have experience in programming, our department offers courses to meet your needs and challenge your abilities.
